SAFI means |

SAFI is a casual dining fusion restaurant inspired by the interconnected cultures of the African Diaspora. We create modern afro-centric cuisine with influences from West Africa, the West Indies and Latin America. The restaurant combines an electrifying atmosphere with indigenous and intricately crafted wood and contemporary design pieces.

Come eat fam

At SAFI, we employ the farm-to-table approach which utilizes sustainable strategies, ensuring that our cooking ingredients are sourced from local farms. This allows us to provide the best quality food at a reduced cost to the consumers.
